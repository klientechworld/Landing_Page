About us

Expertise, combined with quality: The essence of beauty.

AFermosa Brows and Lashes Services, established in September 2020 by Ms. Laiza B. Malate, is a premier beauty destination located in Tanza, Cavite. Specializing in semi-permanent makeup, eyelash extensions, and eyebrow artistry, Fermosa is dedicated to enhancing the natural beauty of its clients with precision and care. Our team of skilled professionals delivers personalized beauty services tailored to individual preferences, ensuring each client leaves feeling confident and satisfied. With a commitment to excellence, Fermosa combines traditional beauty techniques with innovative approaches, providing a seamless and exceptional experience. As we transition from manual booking methods via texting and Facebook, our new web-based booking and facial feature recommendation system will further streamline our services, offering a modern and convenient solution for our valued clients
