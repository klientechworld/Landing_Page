Laser Hair Removal

A medical procedure that uses concentrated beams of light (laser) to target and damage hair follicles, inhibiting future hair growth. It's effective for larger areas and provides long-term hair reduction.

Laser Dark Spots

Laser dark spot removal, or laser pigmentation removal, uses specialized lasers to reduce or eliminate dark spots, hyperpigmentation, and uneven skin tone by targeting excess melanin in the skin.

Carbon Peel Laser

A carbon peel laser procedure rejuvenates skin by applying carbon paste to absorb impurities, then using a laser to remove it, improving texture, tone, and stimulating collagen production.

Facial treatment

Warts Removal

Warts removal is the procedure of eliminating benign skin growths caused by HPV using methods like over-the-counter treatments, freezing, burning, laser therapy, or surgery, tailored to the type and location of the wart.

Radio Frequency

A radiofrequency procedure uses controlled heat from radiofrequency energy to tighten skin, reduce wrinkles, and improve skin texture without surgery.

Microneedling

Microneedling is a cosmetic procedure using fine needles to create micro-injuries in the skin, stimulating collagen production and improving skin texture, scars, wrinkles, and overall tone.

Luxury Eyelashes
